How to Play Bulls and Cows

Objective: Guess your opponent's secret number in the fewest attempts possible.

Rules:

  • Each digit in the number must be unique (no duplicates)
  • After each guess, you'll receive feedback in the form of Bulls and Cows
  • Bull: Correct digit in the correct position
  • Cow: Correct digit but in the wrong position

Example:

Secret number: 4271

Your guess: 1234

Feedback: 1 Bull (digit 2) and 2 Cows (digits 1 and 4)

Game Modes:

  • Human vs Human: Two players take turns guessing each other's numbers
  • Human vs Computer: You guess the computer's randomly generated number

Difficulty Levels:

  • Easy: 3-digit numbers
  • Normal: 4-digit numbers
  • Hard: 5-digit numbers
